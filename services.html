Service catalog

Pick the layer you need
covered — or all of them.

Every engagement starts as one of four service lines. Most clients end up combining two or three.

SERVICE 01

Cloud & Microsoft 365

Full administration of your Microsoft 365 tenant and Azure subscription — licensing, identity, mailboxes, and the cloud infrastructure your team relies on daily.

  • Microsoft 365 tenant administration
  • Entra ID identity & MFA enforcement
  • Azure VM & resource provisioning
  • License audits & cost optimization
  • Exchange Online & SharePoint management
  • Backup & retention policy setup
SERVICE 02

Servers & networking

On-premises infrastructure that needs to stay reliable — Windows Server environments, office networking, and the cabling and hardware underneath it all.

  • Windows Server setup & patching
  • LAN, Wi-Fi & access point deployment
  • Firewall & network security config
  • UPS & power resilience planning
  • Remote desktop & VPN access setup
  • Site-to-site connectivity
SERVICE 03

Helpdesk & on-site support

The day-to-day layer — tickets, hardware faults, and the calls that come in when something just isn't working.

  • Remote helpdesk ticket resolution
  • Laptop & desktop hardware repair
  • New starter device provisioning
  • Loan device management
  • On-site dispatch for hardware issues
  • Peripheral & printer support
SERVICE 04

Security & compliance

Reducing the chance of an incident in the first place — access control, account hygiene, and the policies that keep both audits and attackers satisfied.

  • MFA rollout & enforcement
  • Role-based access reviews
  • Compromised account response
  • Security & compliance reporting
  • Password policy & hygiene audits
  • Data loss prevention setup
