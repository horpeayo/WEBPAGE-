How we work

From first call to closed ticket

A consistent path for every request, whether it's a password reset or a full network rollout.

01

Log the request

Submit a ticket by email, portal, or phone. Critical issues get triaged immediately.

02

Diagnose remotely first

Most issues are resolved over remote desktop or a call — no waiting for a site visit unless hardware is involved.

03

Fix, document, confirm

We resolve the issue, log what was done, and confirm with you before closing the ticket.

04

Flag the pattern

Recurring issues get escalated into a permanent fix, not just repeated patching.
